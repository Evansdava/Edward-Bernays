Summary

This is Edward Bernays, an Austrian American and "double nephew" of Sigmund Freud (who was his mother's brother and his father's sister's husband). Though he started his career as a journalist with an agriculture degree from Cornell, his real work was in the field of public relations, which he revolutionized with techniques based on the work of his Austrian uncle which are still in use today.
